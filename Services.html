Skydiving

Here at Harry and Harriet's, we offer an immersive and vigorating skydiving experience. There are trained professionals who can skydive with customers that are more inexperienced. Waivers and instructions will be given in more detail after making an appointment. Making an appointment is free; however, signing up for an appointment and not showing up with no intent to cancel will result in a fee. To get a better idea of what is offered, you can watch this video that features a customer skydiving for the first time.

Solo Skydiving Requirements:
  1. Sign up for 25 monitored free fall jumps
  2. Complete said 25 free fall jumps successfully. In other words, any health concerns or related issues that occur during the fall will cause the fall to not count and will require further examination.
  3. Have proof of completion of 25 free fall jumps.
  4. Obtain your skydiving A-license package.

Health Concerns- Consult with your doctor before attempting to skydive if you experience any of the following:
  • Hypertension/High Blood Pressure
  • Age-Related Osteoporosis
  • Mobility/Flexibility Issues
  • Weigh over 260 lbs.
  • Diabetes
  • Joint Issues
  • Back Problems
